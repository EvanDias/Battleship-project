1) Each player have a number of ships( depending on the size of the board) to place somewhere on their board(random or manual). The ships can only be placed vertically or horizontally.
2) Once the guessing begins, the players may not move the ships;
3) The ships are: Solo (occupies 1 space), Dual (occupies 2 spaces), Trial (occupies 3 spaces), Quad (occupies 4 spaces) and T_Guy (occupies 5 spaces).
4) Player's take turns guessing by calling out the coordinates (x and y).
5) If player hits a boat coordinate, he can play again until he fails. If he hists all boat coordinates, the boat is sunk.
6) If player miss a boat coordinate, is the opposing player's turn.
7) The game  end when a player's boats are all sunk.
